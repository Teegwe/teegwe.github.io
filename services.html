Our Services

We offer a wide range of services to meet your bakery needs.

Custom Cake Orders

Celebrate special moments with our custom-made cakes tailored to your specifications. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, or any occasion, we design cakes to make your event memorable.

Turnaround Time: 48 hours for standard orders, 7 days for detailed custom designs.

Catering for Events

We offer catering services for events of all sizes. From small parties to large corporate events, our baked goods are sure to impress your guests. Choose from a variety of pastries, cakes, cookies, and more.

Contact us for a personalized catering plan that fits your event needs.

Bakery Delivery Service

Enjoy the comfort of having our delicious baked goods delivered right to your door. We offer delivery within a 10-mile radius of our bakery, ensuring your treats arrive fresh and on time.

Delivery Hours: 9 AM - 6 PM, Monday to Saturday.

Delivery Fee: $5 for orders under $50, free delivery for orders above $50.

Wholesale Orders

We supply fresh baked goods to local cafes, restaurants, and other businesses. If you're looking for a reliable bakery partner, we offer wholesale pricing on bulk orders.

Minimum Order: 50 items. Contact us for more details on pricing and options.

Baking Classes

Join our interactive baking classes and learn how to bake your favorite treats! Whether you're a beginner or an experienced baker, we offer classes for all skill levels.

Class Schedule: Every Saturday from 10 AM to 12 PM.

Price: $40 per person (includes all ingredients and equipment).
